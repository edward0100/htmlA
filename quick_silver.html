Quicksilver

Quicksilver, a Marvel Comics character, possesses superhuman speed. His speed has been estimated and depicted in various comic book stories and adaptations. Here are some notable examples:

  • Mach 300-400: Calculated as around 228,350 mph (20,000 mph).
  • Hypersonic speeds: Traveled 347 miles in 3.7 seconds.
  • Time manipulation: Linked to warping time and space in certain storylines.
  • Variable estimates: Ranging from Mach 5 to Mach 400, depending on the source.

Overall, Quicksilver’s speed is depicted as extraordinary and remains a defining characteristic of the character.
