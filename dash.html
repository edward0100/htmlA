Dash | The Incredibles

Dash Parr, the youngest member of the Incredibles family, possesses superhuman speed. His maximum speed is estimated to be around 190 mph (306 km/h) according to the Incredibles video game. However, he has demonstrated the ability to run on water and on horizontal surfaces, such as walls and ceilings, suggesting his speed may be even higher. Additionally, some calculations and estimates from fans and enthusiasts have put his speed as high as 230 mph (370 km/h) or even Mach 1 (approximately 768 mph or 1,236 km/h) when considering his instantaneous acceleration and deceleration.

It’s worth noting that Dash’s speed is still developing, as he is a young superhero, and it’s possible his abilities will increase with age and training. In comparison to other speed-based superheroes, such as The Flash, Dash’s speed is relatively modest, but still impressive for a human.
