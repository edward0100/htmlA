The Flash

According to various sources, including comic book issues and TV show episodes, the Flash’s speed is remarkably high. In the comics, his top speed is estimated to be 13 trillion times the speed of light, allowing him to travel vast distances in a short time. For example, he can move an entire city to a safe location in an instant, a feat that would take millions of years at the speed of light.

In a specific TV episode, Barry Allen, the Flash, used a Tachyon device to achieve a speed of approximately Mach 13.2 (10,128 mph), making him significantly faster than any human.

It’s worth noting that the Flash’s speed can vary depending on the storyline or continuity, with some instances showing him moving at incredible velocities, such as 9,400,000 million times the speed of light, while others depict him at more moderate speeds. However, his overall speed remains incredibly high, making him the fastest superhero in the DC Universe.
