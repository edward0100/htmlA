Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic the Hedgehog’s speed has been a topic of debate among fans and analysts. While his name suggests he can reach sonic speeds (Mach 1, approximately 768 mph or 1,236 km/h), actual in-game data and calculations reveal a more modest pace.

In the original Sonic the Hedgehog game (1991), his maximum speed is estimated to be around 10 meters per second (33 feet per second), which is relatively fast but not supersonic.

In Sonic Unleashed (2008), his top speed is calculated to be around 80 meters per second (262 feet per second), still impressive but not quite supersonic. There is no official explanation for Sonic’s speed within the mainline, canon timeline of Sonic games. Some sources attribute his speed to his “Power Sneakers” shoes, while others suggest it’s due to his natural ability.
